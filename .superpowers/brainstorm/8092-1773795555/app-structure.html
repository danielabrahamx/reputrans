How should REPUTRANS be structured?

The current 5-step wizard works technically — but what should the product feel like?

A

Linear Wizard (improved current)

Keep the 5-step flow, but make each step feel real: show the master key at Step 1 (like MetaMask onboarding), let users enter custom stats at Step 2, and end with a shareable proof certificate instead of a raw tx hash.

Best for: Fastest to ship · Judges can walk through it in 2 mins · Stays close to current code
B

Identity Dashboard

After registration, users land on a personal dashboard showing their identity (with their key), their credentials, and their proofs. The 5 steps become actions on the dashboard, not a one-time wizard.

Best for: Feels like a real product · User has a "home" · Can issue multiple credentials over time
My Identity My Credentials My Proofs
C

Two-Sided Product

Split the app into two roles: Driver (generates and holds their proof) and Insurer/Verifier (receives and verifies the proof). The driver sends a link; the insurer opens it and sees only what they're allowed to see.

Best for: Most powerful demo · Shows the full privacy story · Mirrors real B2B product vision
Driver Portal Verifier Portal
